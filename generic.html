Tracking Child Deaths In China and India

The visualization demonstrates filtering of data by selecting and comparing data from two countries. Additionally, user can interact with the visualization. Hovering over the traces will reveal more information at that given point.

Top Five Countries Recording Deaths Among Children Less Than 5 Years Of Age

The visualization demonstrates selection or slice of data to include countries with greatest number of child deaths for the given time period. Again, the visualization is dynamic and allows interaction from the user. Stop and play buttons are provided for the user as well as zoom, pan, and ability to download image as a png file.
